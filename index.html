About Me

After acquiring my Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering, I started my professional journey as a Technical Support Specialist, focusing on helping people adopt tech solutions and leverage technology to improve productivity in the workplace. I discovered that through programming, I could have the ability to create new solutions to various problems across a wide range of industries. Now, I am a Full Stack Engineer, specializing in building Ruby on Rails and React/Redux web applications.

In my free time, I love to play chess, use computational power to solve problems (lately, I'm into Project Euler), and spend time with my amazing family.
